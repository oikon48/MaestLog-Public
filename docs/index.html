Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start logging my first symphony experience?

Simply open MaestLog and tap the "+" button to create a new entry. Fill in details about the composer, symphony, performance, and your personal thoughts.

Is my data synchronized across devices?

Your data is stored securely and can be accessed across your devices when signed in to the same account.

Can I export my symphony journal?

Yes, you can export your entries in various formats for backup or sharing purposes through the app settings.
