Data Handling Policy

How we handle your data in MaestLog

Data Storage

All your concert logs are stored locally on your device using Apple's SwiftData framework. While concert data stays on your device, we use RevenueCat for subscription management and Apple Sign In for authentication, which involves minimal external communication.

Data Collection

We do not collect personal information or usage analytics for concert data. However, for premium features (background customization), we collect subscription purchase history via RevenueCat and minimal authentication information when using Apple Sign In.

Data Sharing

Concert record data is not shared with third parties. However, for payment processing we share purchase history with RevenueCat Inc. (US), and for authentication we share authentication data with Apple Inc. (various countries). Both companies comply with appropriate privacy protection standards. RevenueCat Privacy Policy: https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy

Data Security

Your data is protected by iOS's built-in security features including device encryption when enabled. We recommend using device passcode and Face ID/Touch ID for additional security.

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data. You can export, delete, or modify your concert logs at any time through the app. Data deletion is permanent and cannot be recovered.
