About MaestLog

MaestLog is your personal symphony journal app designed for documenting and organizing classical music experiences. Whether you're attending concerts, listening to recordings, or studying compositions, MaestLog helps you capture your musical journey with detailed notes, ratings, and cherished memories.

Key Features

Musical Entries

Log your symphony experiences with detailed notes about composers, performers, and your personal impressions.

Rating System

Rate your experiences and build a personal database of your favorite classical music moments.

Timeline View

Track your musical journey over time and see how your tastes and preferences evolve.

Search & Organization

Easily find recorded pieces by composer, genre, rating, and organize your musical experiences.

Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

Q: How do I start logging my first symphony experience?
A: Simply open MaestLog and tap the "+" button to create a new entry. Fill in details about the composer, symphony, performance, and your personal thoughts and impressions.
Q: Is my data synchronized across devices?
A: Yes, your data is stored securely in the cloud and can be accessed across your devices when signed in to the same account.
Q: Can I export my symphony journal?
A: Yes, you can export your entries in various formats (PDF, CSV, JSON) for backup or sharing purposes through the app settings menu.
Q: What should I do if the app won't start or runs slowly?
A: First, try force-closing the app and restarting it. If the problem persists, restart your device or contact our support team for assistance.
Q: What if my data disappears or gets lost?
A: If you're signed in to your account, your data is automatically synchronized to the cloud. For local data only, we recommend regular backups. Contact support if you experience data loss.
